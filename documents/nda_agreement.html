Non-Disclosure Agreement

This NDA is entered into between GlobalTech Inc ("Disclosing Party") and StartupXYZ ("Receiving Party") as of March 1, 2024.

CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION: Receiving Party agrees not to disclose any trade secrets, business plans, technical data, or financial information of Disclosing Party for 5 years.

NON-COMPETE: Receiving Party shall not engage in any business that directly competes with Disclosing Party within a 50-mile radius for 2 years after termination.

NON-SOLICITATION: For 18 months following termination, Receiving Party shall not solicit any employees or clients of Disclosing Party.

REMEDIES: Breach of this NDA shall entitle Disclosing Party to seek injunctive relief and liquidated damages of $500,000.

GOVERNING LAW: This NDA is governed by California law. Jurisdiction is in San Francisco County courts.

REPRESENTATIONS: Both parties represent they have authority to enter this Agreement and are not subject to any restrictions that would prevent performance.

AMENDMENT: This Agreement may only be amended by a written instrument signed by both parties.

PARTIES: The parties are GlobalTech Inc, a Delaware corporation, and StartupXYZ, a California LLC.
