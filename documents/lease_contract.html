Commercial Lease Agreement

PARTIES: Landlord: Property Holdings Ltd. Tenant: Restaurant Group LLC. Date: February 2024.

RENT: Tenant shall pay $15,000 per month on the first day of each month. A grace period of 5 days applies. Late fee of $500 per day thereafter.

LEASE TERM: Initial term of 5 years commencing April 1, 2024. Option to renew for two additional 5-year periods with 6 months prior notice.

MAINTENANCE: Tenant is responsible for interior maintenance. Landlord responsible for structural repairs and roof maintenance.

INSURANCE: Tenant must carry commercial general liability of $3,000,000 and property insurance equal to full replacement cost.

TERMINATION: Landlord may terminate upon 30 days notice for non-payment or material breach. Tenant may terminate with 90 days notice and 3 months rent as penalty.

INDEMNIFICATION: Tenant indemnifies Landlord against all claims arising from Tenant operations on the premises.

FORCE MAJEURE: If premises are rendered unusable by natural disaster, rent obligation is suspended. Either party may terminate if unusable for more than 6 months.

ASSIGNMENT: Tenant may not assign or sublease without Landlord written consent, not to be unreasonably withheld.

GOVERNING LAW: New York law. Disputes in New York Supreme Court.
